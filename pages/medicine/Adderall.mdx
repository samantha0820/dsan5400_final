# Adderall

<div className="three-column-layout">

## Common side effects

1. Increased heart rate  
2. Elevated blood pressure  
3. Insomnia  
4. Loss of appetite  
5. Dry mouth  
6. Nervousness or anxiety  
7. Restlessness  
8. Headache  
9. Stomach pain or nausea  
10. Weight loss  
11. Dizziness  
12. Mood swings or irritability  
13. Sweating  
14. Tremors  
15. Difficulty sleeping (due to its stimulating effects)  
16. Euphoria (feeling of intense happiness)  
17. Dependence or addiction (with long-term use)  
18. Aggression or hostility  
19. Changes in libido or sexual function  
20. Tics or repetitive movements

### Safety signals

**STRONG ASSOCIATION**  
- Increased heart rate  
- Elevated blood pressure  
- Insomnia  

**MODERATE ASSOCIATION**  
- Loss of appetite  
- Dry mouth  
- Nervousness or anxiety  
- Restlessness  

**LOWER ASSOCIATION**  
- Headache  
- Stomach pain or nausea  
- Weight loss  
- Dizziness
